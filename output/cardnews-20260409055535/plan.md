## 1. Card Type
**Storytelling** — 봄의 설렘 → 명소 소개 → 방문 팁으로 이어지는 감성 여행 내러티브

## 2. Magazine Style
**OLIVE** (선택 이유: 벚꽃의 따뜻하고 감성적인 분위기, 노스탤지아, 아날로그 필름 감성이 OLIVE의 빈티지·개인적·따뜻한 톤과 완벽하게 일치. 봄꽃의 섬세하고 서정적인 아름다움을 가장 잘 담아낼 수 있는 스타일.)

### 색상 팔레트 (OLIVE)
- Primary: `#8B7B6B` (warm brown)
- Background: `#F0E8D5` (aged paper)
- Accent: `#7B9E6B` (olive green)
- Warm: `#D4A574` (warm tan)
- Rust: `#B5704A` (rust)
- Text overlay: white `#FAFAFA` on dark images

## 5. Image Art Direction (상세)

### Card 01 — Cover
- Composition: FullBleed, subject in upper 2/3, lower 35% dark for text
- Lighting: OLIVE intimate analog — warm golden hour, soft backlight through cherry blossom branches
- Camera: 35mm wide, environmental, cherry tree canopy fills frame
- Color: OLIVE palette — warm cream, olive green shadows, soft pink blossoms
- Subject: Cherry blossom avenue (벚꽃 가로수길), petals in air, warm light filtering through branches

### Card 02 — Body
- Composition: FullBleed, people in upper/mid area, lower 35% for text overlay
- Lighting: Warm afternoon diffused light, soft even shadows
- Camera: 50mm, candid energy
- Color: Warm portrait film, slightly warm overexposed highlights
- Subject: People picnicking under cherry blossoms (backs visible), spring picnic scene, petals falling

### Card 03 — Closing
- Composition: FullBleed, close-up fills frame, lower 35% darker for text
- Lighting: Warm intimate, soft side light, slightly overexposed
- Camera: 85mm macro-ish, shallow depth of field
- Color: OLIVE vintage — aged paper tones, warm rust and green accents
- Subject: Cherry blossom petals fallen on ground or floating on water surface, close-up detail
